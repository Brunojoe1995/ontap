= Aggregate creation workflow
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Creating aggregates provides storage to volumes on your system. Beginning with ONTAP 9.2, you can let ONTAP recommend aggregate configurations for your system (auto-provision). If the auto-provision method is not available or appropriate in your environment, you can configure aggregates manually.
